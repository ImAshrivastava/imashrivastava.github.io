Aman Shrivastava

Part-time Genius, Full-time Me

Breaking rules, not just code.


Namaste, and oh Hi! I am Aman—definitely not your average alpha male. I am an international student in Canada. I study, I write hell amount of code, I build what you imagine, I mentor, I work when you sleep, and above everything else, I serve a lot people as a friend—selflessly and tirelessly.

In Canada, I advise and build for the Thingery which is a startup building automated community lending libraries across Vancouver. Once I am done with classes, I come along with a bunch of motivated individuals to prepare sophisticated cleanrooms for major and largest biotech companies in Canada manufacturing the medicines that people use to survive everyday.

In India, I’m growing a seed-stage startup aiming to compete with giants in the electric vehicle space, driving toward a sustainable future.Through another joint venture, I’m helping launch powerful tools for the next generation of leaders in schools across India—reaching students in Atal Tinkering Labs, Incubation Centres, Colleges, and Universities.Some know me as a nerd, others know me for being generous. We have just started investing in a non-profit that you may come across soon in India. Hope you donate and stand with me. We are in this together!

Previously, I have served for large scientific collaborations, award winning and government funded startups as well as countless independent projects at all level across domains. I’ve mentored generations of students, many of whom went on to win prestigious recognitions or complete their undergraduate thesis under my guidance.

I believe this world truly stands on the shoulders of giants—and some of their names are on my website. I am simply a reflection of them, carrying forward their legacy and pushing boundaries beyond expectations. I hope you’ll stand with us in times of need, and let’s all remain grateful for those who make us who we are.
