Autonomous IoT-Enabled Community Animal Welfare Platform
Aman Shrivastava
Anubhav Goyal
Kenneth Thewijaya
Mohamed Abdelmagid
An autonomous, non-profit platform designed for the care and welfare of stray animals integrates IoT-enabled stations to deliver food, water, health monitoring, and hygiene services. Each station is equipped with systems such as automated dispensers, sterilization units, and cameras, and instrumented with a network of sensors. Edge computing modules support real-time animal identification and preliminary health assessments, while a centralized cloud database manages sensor events, resource inventories, animal profiles, and records of staff and veterinary partners.
