Indian Trademark Application No. 5817700
Device Mark
2024
Certificate of Registration
The trademark is registered under Class 9, covering a wide array of electronic and digital products including mobile phone accessories such as chargers, power banks, USB cables, earphones, headphones, and smartwatches. It also includes cutting-edge computing and input devices like keyboards, computer mice, and various types of styluses tailored for touch-screen devices.

Our trademark extends to advanced technology categories such as gesture recognition software, virtual reality glasses, biometric devices (including scanners, locks, and authentication systems), as well as GPS-based systems and software. INGENUITIVE® also covers a broad range of electronics for data processing, sound and image reproduction, home automation, and RFID technology.

Additionally, the registration includes a range of scientific and monitoring instruments, fire safety devices, educational tools, and computing apparatus, underscoring the versatility and innovation that defines the INGENUITIVE® brand.

Published in Trademark Journal No. 2121-0, dated 11/09/2023, this certification highlights our brand's distinct identity and legal protection.

INGENUITIVE®
Indian Trademark Application No. 5817701
Word Mark
2023
Certificate of Registration
The brand name INGENUITIVE® is officially registered as a Word Mark under Class 9, solidifying its legal protection and distinct presence in the rapidly evolving domain of consumer electronics and digital technologies.

This registration covers a diverse range of electronic products and smart solutions including mobile phone chargers, power banks, USB cables, earphones, headphones, keyboards, smartwatches, and styluses tailored for touch-screen devices. It extends to gesture recognition software, speakers, electric sockets, computer peripherals, and other everyday tech essentials that power modern digital lifestyles.

The mark also includes sophisticated systems such as home automation technologies, GPS devices and software, RFID tags, and a comprehensive suite of biometric technologies—from scanners and locks to identity verification systems. It further encompasses virtual reality glasses, sensors, weather instruments, fire alarms, and advanced monitoring tools.

Additionally, INGENUITIVE® is recognized under categories essential to both professional and personal tech use—such as data processing systems, apparatus for sound and image reproduction, magnetic data carriers, vending machines, and fire safety equipment.

Published in Trademark Journal No. 2107-0 on 05/06/2023, this word mark registration ensures exclusive rights to the INGENUITIVE® name across a broad spectrum of electronics and smart technology domains. It reflects our mission to deliver intuitive, high-impact innovations that integrate seamlessly into daily life.
